DRIVE SMART



It is an intelligent system that makes use of off-the-shelves sensors and equipments to provide a solution that can potentially greatly improve the safety of a vehicle driver.

The device alerts the driver when a traffic rule is violated, or can potentially be violated down the road. It also has a logging facility which the driver can access to view ones performance.

FEATURES


  • Speed limit determination based on zones.

  • Traffic signal detection

  • Yellow line crossing detection

  • Real-Time audio alertion to driver

  • Logging of events
